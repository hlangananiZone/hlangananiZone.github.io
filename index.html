Who We Are & Why We Started

Hlanganani Mentorship is a purpose-driven trading and financial literacy platform aimed at empowering everyday individuals with the skills, mindset, and support needed to navigate the markets confidently. We started to close the knowledge gap, provide accessible mentorship, and build a united community of disciplined, growth-focused traders.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Hlanganani Mentorship?

A financial mentorship platform that provides structured trading education, live sessions, and community support to traders at all levels.

2. Who is it for?

It's for beginners, intermediate, and experienced traders looking to sharpen their skills, gain confidence, and trade consistently.

3. How do I join?

Once you've reviewed the mentorship tiers and chosen a suitable plan, follow the registration link provided. You will be guided through the steps to enroll and complete payment.

3. How do I join?

Choose your preferred tier and contact us via WhatsApp or the contact form for payment and onboarding instructions.

4. What do I get each month?

Depending on your tier, you get market analysis, live calls, PDFs, mentorship, and exclusive community access.

5. Can I upgrade later?

Yes. You can move to a higher tier anytime to access more features and support.

6. Are there refunds?

Since this is a knowledge-based service, we don’t offer refunds — but you can cancel anytime before the next billing cycle.
