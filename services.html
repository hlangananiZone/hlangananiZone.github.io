Basic Forex Information

Forex trading involves buying and selling currency pairs, where prices move in response to supply and demand. Candlestick charts display price action over time, helping traders identify trends, reversals, and market sentiment. Each candlestick shows the open, high, low, and close prices for a given period. Mastering chart reading is key to making informed trading decisions.
